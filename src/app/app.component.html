ABOUT ME

The Odeyssey of life is the unveiling of destiny

Shannon Ware began computing in the era of Commodore 64 and the Apple II. In high school we learned Pascal on Macs, but instead of going on to C++ programming, he went head-long into the study of Japanese language and culture. After working as a translator, etc., for many years, around 2006 he renewed his interest in programming as a Python hobbyist. Still, the bills were paid working as Japanese language tech support until he entered the George Brown College game design program.

Today, Shannon enjoys spinning Angular CLI applications that are fit for purpose. The power of the JavaScript and Node ecosystems is exciting, and every day provides new opportunities to build long lasting applications.

Shannon Ware


Technology moves forward at a more or less predictable rate. It is also predictable and well known that design does not move forward overall. By 'technology' we mean the increased power of digital devices. Faster processors and bigger memory in the future is a given, but for an ever improving user interface, it is a given that it will not occur.

Nevertheless, user interface design tools are more robust than ever before, and application build tools are delivering on the promise of platform integration. Shannon Ware is proud to offer solutions built with well-established open source technologies. These select technologies are backed by both open source technology communities and major corporations. The technology is more secure, more extensible, and more accessible than ever before.
